About the author

Doug Hanson is an award-winning, best-selling author, and highly sought-after speaker. He makes every event a hit with his rapid pace of stories, humor, metaphors, and exercises, all tailored to the client’s event objectives, which allows him to connect with virtually any group in any industry. Doug has entertained, inspired, and motivated over one million people during his 25 years as a speaker, giving over 1,200+ presentations in 15 different countries, including seven consecutive years at Tony Robbins’ Life Mastery University in Hawaii, and the NFL’s Super Bowl XXXVIII, where Doug addressed more than 10,000 employees, staffers, and city officials at the NFL’s pregame pep rally in Houston’s NRG Stadium.
